Seven-Day Japan Itinerary

Day One - Tokyo:
Morning: Arrive in Tokyo, check into hotel
Afternoon: Explore Shibuya Crossing and Harajuku
Evening: Welcome dinner in Shinjuku

Day Two - Tokyo:
Morning: Senso-ji Temple and Asakusa area
Afternoon: Akihabara electronics district
Evening: Tokyo Tower visit

Day Three - Tokyo to Kyoto:
Morning: Shinkansen bullet train to Kyoto
Afternoon: Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
Evening: Gion district geisha area

Day Four - Kyoto:
Morning: Fushimi Inari Shrine
Afternoon: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
Evening: Traditional kaiseki dinner

Day Five - Kyoto to Osaka:
Morning: Kiyomizu-dera Temple
Afternoon: Train to Osaka, check into hotel
Evening: Dotonbori street food tour

Day Six - Osaka:
Morning: Osaka Castle
Afternoon: Kuromon Ichiba Market
Evening: Osaka Aquarium and Tempozan Ferris Wheel

Day Seven - Osaka to Tokyo:
Morning: Shopping at Shinsaibashi
Afternoon: Shinkansen back to Tokyo
Evening: Final dinner in Tokyo before departure

Notes:
- Purchase a seven-day Japan Rail Pass before arrival
- Make hotel reservations in advance
- Consider purchasing tickets for major attractions online
- Check weather forecast and pack accordingly
